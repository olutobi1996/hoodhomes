Areas Covered

Cambridge City Centre

The city centre blends historic architecture with modern convenience, offering a lifestyle that is both dynamic and charming. From grand Victorian terraces and elegant Edwardian homes to stylish new-build apartments, the centre attracts a wide mix of residents including academics, professionals, investors, and students. With world-famous colleges, museums, cultural landmarks, and a thriving café and dining scene on the doorstep, living here means being immersed in the unique character of Cambridge. Excellent transport connections and access to employment hubs make it one of the most desirable areas in the region.

South Cambridge

South Cambridge offers a quieter pace of life with attractive residential streets, excellent schools, and proximity to Addenbrooke’s Hospital and the Biomedical Campus. It is particularly popular with medical professionals, academics, and families who value the strong sense of community and abundance of green spaces. Local amenities include shops, cafés, and easy access to countryside walks, while excellent bus and train links make commuting straightforward. With strong demand from both buyers and renters, property in South Cambridge has proven to be a sound long-term investment.

Eddington

Eddington is Cambridge’s flagship modern development, designed with sustainability and community at its heart. Built with eco-friendly housing, energy-efficient infrastructure, and landscaped green spaces, it is a forward-thinking district that sets a new standard for urban living. Families, academics, and young professionals are drawn to its new schools, cycle-friendly layout, and strong links to the University of Cambridge. With modern design and future-proof planning, Eddington is fast becoming one of the most distinctive neighbourhoods in the city.

North Cambridge

North Cambridge combines traditional terraced streets with exciting new residential developments, making it attractive to a diverse range of buyers and tenants. Its appeal has grown significantly with the opening of Cambridge North Station, providing quick connections to London and beyond. The area also benefits from local shops, schools, and community spaces, alongside future regeneration projects that continue to add value. With growing demand from commuters, professionals, and investors, North Cambridge is one of the city’s fastest-rising areas.

Milton & Waterbeach

Just to the north of Cambridge, Milton and Waterbeach are thriving communities that combine riverside charm with excellent transport links. Both areas are well-connected by road and rail, making them particularly attractive to commuters, while offering larger homes and more outdoor space than the city centre. Local parks, schools, and village amenities provide a family-friendly environment, and ongoing development plans in Waterbeach promise to bring even greater opportunities. With a balance of traditional village character and exciting growth, they continue to draw strong interest from buyers and investors.

The Villages

Surrounding Cambridge are a wealth of picturesque villages, each offering something unique to buyers seeking charm and space. Grantchester, Coton, Comberton, and Barton are just a few examples where residents enjoy a countryside lifestyle within minutes of the city. Properties range from historic period cottages and barn conversions to modern family homes, with many set in scenic locations. For those wanting tranquillity without sacrificing convenience, the villages provide the perfect balance of rural beauty and access to Cambridge’s opportunities.

Histon & Impington

Histon and Impington are neighbouring villages that have grown into some of the most sought-after locations around Cambridge. Known for their excellent schools, welcoming community, and strong transport connections, they appeal especially to families and professionals. The villages have a vibrant local scene with independent shops, pubs, and regular events that foster a close-knit atmosphere. With a diverse mix of housing — from period properties to modern developments — they offer something for every stage of life while maintaining consistently high demand.
