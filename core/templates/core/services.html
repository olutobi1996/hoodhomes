Areas Covered

Cambridge City Centre

The city centre blends historic architecture with modern convenience, offering a dynamic lifestyle. From Victorian terraces to stylish new-builds, it attracts a wide mix of residents including academics, professionals, and students. Excellent transport and access to employment hubs make it highly desirable.

South Cambridge

South Cambridge offers a quieter pace of life with attractive streets, excellent schools, and proximity to Addenbrooke’s Hospital. Popular with families and professionals, local amenities, green spaces, and strong transport links make it a highly sought-after area.

Eddington

Eddington is a modern, sustainable development with eco-friendly housing and landscaped green spaces. Families and professionals are drawn to its new schools, cycle-friendly layout, and strong links to the University of Cambridge.

North Cambridge

North Cambridge combines traditional streets with new developments. With Cambridge North Station, shops, schools, and ongoing regeneration, it is a highly desirable area for commuters and investors.

Milton & Waterbeach

Milton and Waterbeach offer riverside charm, larger homes, and outdoor space. Excellent transport links and local amenities make these thriving communities ideal for families and commuters.

The Villages

Surrounding Cambridge, picturesque villages like Grantchester and Comberton offer charm, space, and rural tranquility, with historic properties and modern homes within easy reach of the city.

Histon & Impington

Histon and Impington combine excellent schools, community, and transport links. From period homes to modern developments, they offer highly desirable living for families and professionals.
