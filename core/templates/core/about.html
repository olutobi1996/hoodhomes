About Hood Homes

Cambridge Estate & Letting Experts

Our Story

Hood Homes was founded with a clear mission — to bring transparency, professionalism, and care back into the Cambridge property market. As a multi-award-winning estate and letting agent, we’ve always called Cambridge home, giving us deep insight into the city and surrounding villages.

With years of combined experience, our approach blends modern technology with traditional values. We prioritise long-term relationships, client satisfaction, and tailored advice over quick wins.

Our Services

Sales

From first impressions to final negotiations, our sales service is crafted to maximise your property’s value. We provide carefully written particulars, professional photography, and detailed plans that highlight what truly matters to buyers.

Lettings

We offer tailored lettings solutions for landlords and tenants alike, ensuring fairness, transparency, and exceptional presentation for every home we manage.

Investment

With our knowledge of the Cambridge market, we advise investors on opportunities that balance long-term growth with strong returns, guiding you every step of the way.

Our Policy

We believe in doing business responsibly. Our environmental policy is centred around reducing waste, promoting energy efficiency, and partnering with like-minded professionals who share our vision for sustainable housing.

Beyond the environment, our policies ensure ethical practices across sales, lettings, and investment. Transparency, fairness, and respect are at the heart of every decision we make.
